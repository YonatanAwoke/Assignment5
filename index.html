HI, I AM YONATAN AWOKE

Lifestyle

My name is Yonatan Awoke. I live in the densely populated Gerji district that is under the metropolitan Bole sub-city. I was
born and raise there so I can say I am familiar with everything that goes around and comes around the town.
I completed my primary school education in a local school called Embut primary school in which both my elder siblings went to. After I passed the eighth grade national examination, I moved to the well known Saint Joseph School. I definitely had spent some good and bad times in highschool. It shaped me into who I am as a person right now.



In 2010, I graduted from highschool with a decent result. I managed to get into Addis Ababa Institute of Technology. I have a passion to creating new things in the sector of Technology. The IT department that I am being enrolled will give me the necessary push to persue my dream as a developer. But I argue that universities should be just more than educational hub. In my view, they should also nurture the youth to become hard working, socialistic and take part in extracurricular activities. One of my strong qualities as a person might be working hard every single day in anything I put my mind to. Although I have a long way to go I try to give my best version of me everyday. So, universities and other huge institutes should take the front stage in shaping the young generation.
